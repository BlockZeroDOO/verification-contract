# Ricardian Clauses

## addwhuser

Adds an account to the wholesale pricing table maintained by the contract.

## rmwhuser

Removes an account from the wholesale pricing table maintained by the contract.

## withdraw

Transfers collected `GFT` tokens from the contract account to the specified recipient.

## quote

Returns the currently applicable price tier for the specified account.

## iswhuser

Returns whether the specified account is currently marked as wholesale.
