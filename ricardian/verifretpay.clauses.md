<h1 class="clause">settoken</h1>

Only the contract account may manage accepted retail payment tokens.

<h1 class="clause">setprice</h1>

Retail tariffs are exact-price tariffs. Payments must match the configured price exactly.

<h1 class="clause">consume</h1>

Only `verifretpay` itself or the downstream `verif` anchoring contract may consume a retail authorization.
